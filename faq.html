Audiobook Speed Calculator FAQ

Answers to common questions about audiobook listening speeds and time savings.

What is the best audiobook speed?

Most listeners find that 1.25x to 1.75x provides a balance between comprehension and efficiency.

Does listening faster reduce comprehension?

Research suggests moderate increases in playback speed have little impact on comprehension for many listeners.

How much time does 2x speed save?

A 20-hour audiobook becomes 10 hours at 2x speed.

Can I use this calculator for podcasts?

Yes. The calculator works for any audio content.

What speed do Audible users typically use?

Many Audible users listen between 1.2x and 2.0x depending on narration style.

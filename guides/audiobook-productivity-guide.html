Audiobook Productivity Guide

One of the easiest ways to increase reading volume is by adjusting playback speed.

Benefits of Faster Playback

  • Finish books sooner
  • Consume more content annually
  • Reduce downtime during commutes
  • Improve focus for some listeners

Example

Listening to 50 twenty-hour audiobooks:

Speed Annual Listening Time
1x 1000 Hours
1.5x 667 Hours
2x 500 Hours

Finding Your Optimal Speed

Increase speed gradually by 0.1x or 0.25x increments until comprehension begins to decline.

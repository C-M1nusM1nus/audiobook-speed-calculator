What Is the Best Audiobook Speed?

The best audiobook speed depends on your goals. Most listeners prefer between 1.25x and 1.75x playback because it improves efficiency while maintaining comprehension.

Recommended Speeds

Goal Speed
Maximum comprehension 1.0x–1.25x
General listening 1.25x–1.75x
Fast learning 1.75x–2.5x
Extreme speed listening 2.5x+

Fiction vs Nonfiction

Fiction often benefits from slower playback to appreciate narration, while nonfiction can usually be consumed faster.

Audiobook Listening Time Calculator Guide

Changing playback speed dramatically affects the amount of time required to finish an audiobook.

Time Savings by Playback Speed

Speed 20 Hour Book
1x 20 Hours
1.25x 16 Hours
1.5x 13h 20m
2x 10 Hours
3x 6h 40m

Formula

Listening Time = Original Duration ÷ Playback Speed

Example Calculation

A 15-hour audiobook played at 1.5x speed takes approximately 10 hours to complete.
